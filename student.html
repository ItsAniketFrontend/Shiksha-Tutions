One to One
Attention

A Home tutor can provide one-on-one attention to the student in a home tutoring class, which is often not possible in a traditional classroom setting. Individual attention can help students better understand concepts and ask questions.

Safe Learning Environment

Parents may feel more at ease with their child learning in a safe, familiar environment such as their own home rather than a new or unknown location. That's why Modiv's Home Tutors Take Classes at Student's place.

Convenience and Flexibility

Home Tutoring allows the student and tutor to meet at a time that is convenient for both of them. This means they can work around other obligations like school, work, and extracurricular activities.

Greater
Engagement

When students receive individualised attention from a tutor, they often feel more engaged and motivated. As a result,This can help them stay focused and attentive during lessons, as well as retain more information.
